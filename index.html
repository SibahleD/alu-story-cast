About StoryCast

Making storytelling accessible to everyone

Our Mission

StoryCast is dedicated to creating and sharing compelling audio and video stories that are fully accessible to all users, regardless of ability. We believe everyone deserves equal access to powerful storytelling.

Accessibility Features

Full Transcripts

Every audio and video story includes a complete written transcript, making content accessible to deaf and hard-of-hearing users.

Closed Captions

All video content includes synchronized closed captions with proper timing and speaker identification.

Keyboard Navigation

Full keyboard support with visible focus indicators for users who cannot use a mouse.

Screen Reader Support

Semantic HTML and ARIA attributes ensure compatibility with screen readers and other assistive technologies.

Color Contrast

All text meets WCAG AA contrast requirements for users with low vision or color blindness.

Responsive Design

Optimized for all devices and screen sizes, from mobile phones to desktop computers.
